The '''2012–13 [[Football League Two]]''' (referred to as the '''[[Npower (UK)|Npower]] Football League Two''' for sponsorship reasons) will be the ninth season of the league under its current title and twentieth season under its current league division format. It will start on 18 August 2012 and finish on 27 April 2013 with all matches that day kicking off simultaneously.
