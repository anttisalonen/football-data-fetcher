The '''2012–13 [[Football League One]]''' (referred to as the '''[[Npower (UK)|Npower]] Football League One''' for sponsorship reasons) is the ninth season of the league under its current title and twentieth season under its current league division format. The season began on 18 August 2012 and will end on 27 April 2013.

==Changes from last season==
===Team changes===
====To League One====
;Relegated from the [[2011–12 Football League Championship|Championship]]
* [[Portsmouth F.C.|Portsmouth]]
* [[Coventry City F.C.|Coventry City]]
* [[Doncaster Rovers F.C.|Doncaster Rovers]]

;Promoted from [[2011–12 Football League Two|League Two]]
* [[Swindon Town F.C.|Swindon Town]]
* [[Shrewsbury Town F.C.|Shrewsbury Town]]
* [[Crawley Town F.C.|Crawley Town]]
* [[Crewe Alexandra F.C.|Crewe Alexandra]]

====From League One====
;Promoted to the [[2012-13 Football League Championship|Championship]]
* [[Charlton Athletic F.C.|Charlton Athletic]]
* [[Sheffield Wednesday F.C.|Sheffield Wednesday]]
* [[Huddersfield Town F.C.|Huddersfield Town]]

;Relegated to [[2012-13 Football League Two|League Two]]
* [[Wycombe Wanderers F.C.|Wycombe Wanderers]]
* [[Chesterfield F.C.|Chesterfield]]
* [[Exeter City F.C.|Exeter City]]
* [[Rochdale A.F.C.|Rochdale]]
